The Experience

About This Trip

This is a 12-day journey across both islands of New Zealand, built around the kind of experiences we genuinely love. From Māori culture and glowworm cave rafting in Rotorua to lakeside sauna, group picnics, and slow scenic moments in the South Island — it's a mix of adventure and easy, feel-good travel.

There's also space to level it up with options like a helicopter ride over Mount Cook, bungee jumping in Queenstown, or skydiving in Wanaka. It's not rushed, not touristy — just a well-paced trip that lets you actually enjoy New Zealand the way we do.

"Not rushed, not touristy — just New Zealand the way it should be."

The eighth wonder of the world

Drive through Fiordland's dramatic landscapes to Te Anau, then journey deep into Milford Sound — towering peaks, cascading waterfalls, and mirror-still fiord waters that will leave you speechless.

Included Experiences

Queenstown

Adventure capital of the world

Three nights in the world's adventure capital. Jet boat through canyon walls at full speed, soak in a private Onsen overlooking the Remarkables, then ride the gondola up to Skyline for thrilling luge runs.

Included Experiences

Shotover Jet Onsen Hotpools Skyline + 3 Luge Rides

Wanaka

Serene alpine lake town with iconic views

Two nights beside crystal-clear Lake Wanaka, framed by the Southern Alps. Stroll the lakefront to That Wanaka Tree — NZ's most photographed spot — and explore the quirky Puzzling World.

Included Experiences

Puzzling World That Wanaka Tree Lake Wanaka Lakefront

Lake Tekapo

UNESCO Dark Sky Reserve — world-class stargazing

Two magical nights on the turquoise shores of Lake Tekapo. By day: lupins, the iconic Church of the Good Shepherd, and mountain views. By night: the most spectacular stars on Earth from a UNESCO Dark Sky Reserve.

Included Experiences

Dark Sky Observatory Church of the Good Shepherd Lake Tekapo Lakefront

The Garden City — farewell to New Zealand

End your NZ adventure in Christchurch — the Garden City. Wander the stunning Botanic Gardens, soak in the creative arts district, and savour one final morning before your flight home.

Meet Sagar & Ami

Doubtful Sound · New Zealand

Indian-born Kiwis,
living slow.

We're Sagar and Ami — Indian-born Kiwis who've been living and travelling across New Zealand for the last 10 years.

Two years ago, we left our jobs to travel full-time around the world. Now we're back living in our van, exploring NZ slowly while building a life around travel.

We love simple moments, real experiences, and showing a side of travel that feels honest and doable.

This is our very first group trip — so it's a special one, and we'd love for you to be part of it.

— Sagar & Ami
